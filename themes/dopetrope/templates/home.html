Build Your Own Gaming Console

μbrew Development Kit

The μbrew development kit is a modular framework to build your own entertainment and gaming machine. The kit is based on Buildroot, Qt and V-Play and contains a graphical environment to launch games with gamepad support and a modern code editor and designer for app and game developers.
