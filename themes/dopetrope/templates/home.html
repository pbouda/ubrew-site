Welcome to ZEOMA!

Welcome to the official website of ZEOMA (provisional name).

ZEOMA is the revolution in the world of video game consoles. ZEOMA is a video game console in which you can decide how much hardware power you need. You can decide whether to play at home on your TV or play anywhere. You can decide the type of screen you want. You can choose the OS of the game console. You can even customize the controls of the video game console!

ZEOMA is the first modular handheld game console. ZEOMA is based on the EOMA-68 concept. EOMA-68 is a cartridge (such as GameBoy cartridges) but contains the main hardware (CPU, GPU, RAM, etc.), so just changing the EOMA-68, you can increase the power of your console without buy another video game console!

You can find more information about this project on the About page. If you want to contribute to this project, please visit the Join Us page.
