Build Your Own Gaming Console

μbrew Kit for Embedded Entertainment

The μbrew Kit is a modular framework to build your own entertainment and gaming machine. It is a customizable solution to provide your own app store on your embedded platform. The Kit is based on Buildroot, Systemd and Btrfs and features dependency and package management, a graphical environment to launch apps and an SDK with editor and designer for app developers.
