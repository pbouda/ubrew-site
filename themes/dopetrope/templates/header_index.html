Open & Hackable

ZEOMA is the revolution in the world of video game consoles and based on open hardware. You can decide how much hardware power you need and if you want to play at home on your TV or mobile on the screen you choose. Install any OS. You can even customize the controls to your needs!

Modular

ZEOMA is the first modular handheld game console. ZEOMA is based on the EOMA-68 concept. EOMA-68 is a cartridge that contains the main hardware (CPU, GPU, RAM, etc.) that you plug into the game console, so just by changing the EOMA-68 you can increase the power of your console without buying a new device!

Collaborative

ZEOMA is developed by a friendly community and you are invited to join in. You can find more information about this project on the About page. If you want to contribute to this project, please visit the Join Us page.
