Build

μbrew provides you with everything you need to build your own entertainment machine. We put together easy-to-follow tutorials that let you construct a customized gaming consoles within a few hours. But it doesn't stop with building the hardware. Learn how a modern, embedded operating system and its software stack works and customize your console down to the kernel boot procedure.

Learn

The μbrew gaming platform supports several gaming SDKs out-of-the box. Learn how to build your own games with PyGame or the V-Play game engine, and publish your game on the μbrew Game Store. You will learn how to publish your game on iOS and Android platforms, to reach a wider audience and make money.
